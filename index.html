About Us

A Florida AeroCoast FTC Team

We are the Radical Raiders.
This is our first year participating in the FIRST Tech Challenge. In addition, we are a team consisting of high school students from Collegiate High School at Northwest Florida State College.
Despite our short history, the Radical Raiders are extremely excited to be involved in this opportunity and look forward to many years of continued success.

More About FTC

It’s more than just building robots. FIRST Tech Challenge teams must design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in a head-to-head challenge. Guided by adult coaches and mentors, students learn STEM skills and engineering principles

More About This Year's Season

This year's season theme puts humans and droid allies together to build the tallest towers. FTC teams must push through obstacles that stand in their way to build a future megastructure, finishing the tower with thier own capstone to symbolize their reach into the sky, and dreams of a hopeful future.
